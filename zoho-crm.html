Zoho CRM Interview Questions

Comprehensive guide to help you prepare for Zoho CRM interviews, from basics to advanced scenarios.

Module 1: Basic/Conceptual Questions

Interview Questions:

1.1: What is Zoho CRM and what are its core features?
1.2: What are Leads, Contacts, Accounts, and Deals in Zoho CRM?
1.3: Explain the concept of modules in Zoho CRM.
1.4: How does Zoho CRM differ from other CRM platforms like Salesforce or HubSpot?
1.5: What is the difference between a Standard and a Custom Module?


Module 2: Customization & Setup

Interview Questions:

2.1: How do you customize fields in Zoho CRM?
2.2: What are layouts and how are they used?
2.3: How can you create and use custom views?
2.4: Explain validation rules and field permissions.
2.5: How can you set up picklists and multi-select fields?


Module 3: Automation & Workflows

Interview Questions:

3.1: What are workflows in Zoho CRM and how do they work?
3.2: What are blueprint processes in Zoho CRM?
3.3: How do you create and use custom functions?
3.4: What is the use of assignment rules?
3.5: How are email alerts and task automation handled?


Module 4: Data Management

Interview Questions:

4.1: How do you import and export data in Zoho CRM?
4.2: What are data deduplication tools available in Zoho CRM?
4.3: Explain how data is secured and backed up.
4.4: What is the use of data sharing rules and roles?
4.5: How do profiles and roles differ in CRM?


Module 5: Reports & Dashboards

Interview Questions:

5.1: How do you create a report in Zoho CRM?
5.2: What are the different types of reports available?
5.3: How do dashboards work, and how do you share them?
5.4: Can you schedule reports and alerts?
5.5: What insights can a sales manager get from CRM dashboards?


Module 6: Integration & Extensions

Interview Questions:

6.1: What Zoho apps can be integrated with Zoho CRM?
6.2: How do you connect Zoho CRM with third-party tools?
6.3: What is Zoho Marketplace and how does it help CRM users?
6.4: Can you integrate Zoho CRM with email services like Outlook or Gmail?
6.5: How can you automate tasks using Zoho Flow or Deluge?


Module 7: Advanced/Scenario-Based Questions

Interview Questions:

7.1: A lead is not converting to a deal — what steps would you take?
7.2: A user complains they can’t see a record they created — how do you troubleshoot?
7.3: You want to track ROI of marketing campaigns — how do you do it in Zoho CRM?
7.4: A sales team needs a 4-step approval process for deals — how would you set it up?
7.5: Describe how you’d onboard a new team into Zoho CRM from scratch.


Module 8: Hands-On/Practical Test Ideas

Practical Tasks:

8.1: Create a workflow rule to send an email when a new lead is added.
8.2: Build a custom report showing deals closed last month by each sales rep.
8.3: Set up a blueprint for a 3-stage sales process: Prospect → Negotiation → Closure.
8.4: Create a custom function to auto-update the "Stage" field based on deal amount.
