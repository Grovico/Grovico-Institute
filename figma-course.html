Course Syllabus Overview

This is a brief overview of the syllabus. For more detailed information on each topic, please download the full syllabus.

Download Full Syllabus

Week 1: UX Foundations

- What is UX?
- Difference between UX and UI
- Design Thinking Process
- Principles of Human-Centered Design
- Research Methods, Personas, and Journey Mapping

Week 2: UX Strategy & Architecture

- Site Mapping & Navigation
- Content Structuring
- User Flows & Task Flows
- Advanced Journey Mapping

Week 3: Wireframing & Prototyping

- Sketching & Wireframing
- Low vs High-Fidelity Designs
- Responsive Layouts & Grids
- Prototyping with Figma

Week 4: UI Design Principles

- Color Theory & Typography
- Visual Hierarchy & Consistency
- UI Components (Buttons, Forms, Cards)
- Accessibility Design Basics

Week 5: Design Systems

- What is a Design System?
- Components, Style Guides, Tokens
- Examples from Material Design & Apple HIG

Week 6: Usability & Testing

- Heuristic Evaluation
- A/B Testing & Feedback Analysis
- Iterative Design & Refinement

Week 7: Microinteractions & Motion

- Introduction to Motion in UI
- Microinteractions & Smart Animate in Figma
- Loading, Transition & Feedback States

Week 8: Real-World Projects & Portfolio

- Capstone Project (Full UX/UI Flow)
- Writing Case Studies
- Building a Portfolio (Dribbble, Behance, LinkedIn)
